betwixt Salomon the king of jheruſalē and
Marcolphus that right rude and great of body
was bnt right ſubtyll & wyſe of wyt / and full
of vndreſtādyng. as thereafter folowyng men
ſhall here.
¶Emprentyd at andewerpe by
me M. Gerardleen
